Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

AkademiX is an academic management platform developed exclusively for students of Türk-Alman Üniversitesi (TAÜ). This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices you have. By using AkademiX you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who We Are

AkademiX is an independent student-built project. It is not an official product of Türk-Alman Üniversitesi. Contact: akademix.contact@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google and use AkademiX, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Google account profile: name, email address, profile picture (for identifying your account)
  • Google Classroom data: course list, course work, announcements — read-only, via Google Classroom API
  • Google OAuth tokens: an access token and a refresh token issued by Google, stored server-side so that you stay logged in and so that Classroom data can be refreshed
  • Content you create: uploaded materials (PDFs, links), notes, AI-generated summaries/exams/flashcards, and chat messages you exchange with the AI
  • Technical metadata: timestamps of your activity and basic rate-limiting counters (number of AI requests per day)

We do not collect passwords, payment information, IP addresses for tracking purposes, advertising identifiers, or any analytics data.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to operate the features of AkademiX:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session
  • To display your Google Classroom courses, assignments, and announcements within the app
  • To store materials, notes, and AI outputs you create so they persist across devices
  • To send content to AI providers when you request an AI feature (see Section 5)
  • To enforce fair-use rate limits

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the features you actively use.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

AkademiX is a hosted web application. Your data is stored in the following services:

  • Supabase (EU region) — database and file storage for materials, notes, AI outputs, chat history, and session tokens. supabase.com/privacy
  • Vercel (serverless backend hosting). vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Your browser — some preferences and a cached copy of your materials are kept in localStorage for faster loading

5. AI Providers (Third-Party Processing)

When you use an AI feature, the relevant content (material text, your question, or chat history) is sent to one of the following providers for processing. These providers act as data processors on our behalf and do not use your data to train their models under our current agreements:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — used for summaries, exam generation, and flashcards. anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google (Gemini API) — used for chat, AI Studio, and RAG answers. policies.google.com/privacy
  • Hugging Face — used to create embeddings of uploaded material text for the RAG search feature. huggingface.co/privacy

Content sent to these providers is transmitted over HTTPS and is used only to generate the response you requested.

6. Google Workspace Limited Use Disclosure

AkademiX's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

This means that data obtained from Google APIs (your profile, Classroom courses, course work, and announcements) is only used to provide the features you see in the app. It is not sold, not used for advertising, not used to train AI models, and not shared with third parties except the sub-processors listed in Section 5.

7. Data Retention

  • Session tokens: stored while your session is active. Sessions expire after 7 days of inactivity and are then automatically deleted.
  • User-generated content (materials, notes, AI outputs, chat history): retained until you delete it from the app or until you request account deletion.
  • Google Classroom data: not stored persistently. It is fetched live from Google each time you open the Classroom page.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data — by removing individual items in the app, or by emailing us to delete your entire account
  • Export a copy of your data
  • Revoke Google access at any time via myaccount.google.com/permissions

To exercise any of these rights, email akademix.contact@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Security

All traffic between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS. Sensitive tokens are held only on the server side and are not exposed to the frontend. Database access is restricted by row-level security. Only the account owner can read or modify their own data.

No system is completely secure. If we become aware of a breach that affects your personal data, we will notify affected users without undue delay.

10. Children

AkademiX is intended for university students (typically 18+). We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version. For significant changes we will post a notice in the app.

12. Contact

Questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or requests to exercise your rights:
