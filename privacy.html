Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

AkademiX is an academic management platform developed exclusively for students of Türk-Alman Üniversitesi (TAÜ). This privacy policy explains what data our application collects and how it is handled.

📋 Data We Collect

AkademiX accesses the following data:

  • Google account information (name, email address, profile picture)
  • Google Classroom data (courses, assignments, announcements)
  • Materials and notes uploaded or created within the app
  • AI-generated summaries saved by the user

💾 Data Storage

User data is stored in two ways:

  • Locally — course schedules and preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB
  • Cloud — materials, notes, and AI summaries are synced to our Supabase database so they are accessible across devices
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties
  • No advertising or tracking scripts are used

🔑 Google OAuth

AkademiX uses Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication. The sign-in process is handled entirely through Google's secure infrastructure.

  • Only @stud.tau.edu.tr institutional accounts are accepted
  • Google Classroom data is used solely within the application
  • Access tokens are stored in browser memory and are never sent to our servers

🤖 AI Summarization

The AI summarization feature uses the Anthropic Claude API. When you use this feature:

  • The content of your uploaded document is sent to the Anthropic API for processing
  • No document content is permanently stored by Anthropic beyond the duration of the request
  • For Anthropic's privacy policy, visit: anthropic.com/privacy

🔒 Data Security

  • All communication is encrypted via HTTPS
  • Cloud data is stored securely on Supabase's infrastructure
  • The application contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising code
  • Access is restricted to TAÜ institutional Google accounts

🗑️ Deleting Your Data

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Clear site data from your browser settings to remove all local data
  • To remove cloud-stored data, contact us via GitHub
  • To revoke Google access, visit myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove AkademiX
